Enjoying the Harvest

One of the easiest ways to make food tasier is to use fresh herbs.

My Top Five Herbs

  1. Basil –  You can enjoy it fresh for months during the summer.
  2. Mint –  Easily frozen or dried for winter use and great for drinks.
  3. Rosemary –  Delicious with eggs in the morning or beef stew.
  4. Thyme –  Its frgrance is soft and mellow, yet distinctly unique.
  5. Oregano –  Pizza and pasta all benefit from its earthy flavour.

Homemade Tastes Better

Cherries are in abundance, and this quick and easy jam is a great way to capture their deliciousness for months.

Freezer Cherry Jam

  • 18 cups washed, pitted, roughly pureed sweet cherries
  • 7 cups organic, whole dehydrated cane sugar
  • 3 cups filtered water
  • 10 teaspoons pectin
  1. Add all the pureed cherries into a large bowl.
  2. Add in the sugar. Stir to combine. Taste test its sweetness.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the water to a boil. Add in the pectin and use an immersion blender to combine. Once the pectin is dissolved, remove from heat.
  4. Pour the pectin/water mixture into the cherry mixture.
  5. You'll see the jam begin to set almost immediately. Just keep stirring for a few minutes while it all combines.
  6. Scoop out the jam into freezer-safe containers.
  7. Allow the containers to sit at room temperature for a few hours. After the jam has completely set, transfer it to the freezer.
