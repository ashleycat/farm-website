Eggs

Eggs are the reason most of us begin backyard farming. Who doesn't want those rich, homegrown, deep yellow yolks on our breakfast table? Eggs are an edible testament to what we love about farm life.

Milk

Dairy animals require a bit more commitment, but they also give more than almost any other animal on the farm. They provide us with fresh milk and cream but also the ability to make butter and cheese.

Friends for Life
